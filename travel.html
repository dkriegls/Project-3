Favorite places to travel to

1.Ecuador

Ecuador is great place to travel to because it is so diverse.

The country is small and has a variety of landscapes. From mountains to beaches that are around a 4 hour drive to each other.

Mexico

Mexico has around 32 states that are quite diverse. There is so much to see that I would recommend you take a road trip.

One of my favorite places to visit is Mexico city. Since it is a mega city and has a lot to offer.

Canada

Canada is a huge country to travel to. I enjoy taking road trips there. Since it is only a couple of hours from here

I usually just drive to Toronto or Montreal. But i prefer Toronto.

NYC

Nyc is one of the best places you can visit. It is such a large city full of life.

The food,people, and smell give it some character. I would recommend everyone to visit nyc at least once in their life.
